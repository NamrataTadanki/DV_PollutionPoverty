About the relationship between Pollution and Poverty

Pollution and Poverty by Region

Explore the relationship between poverty and pollution with this interactive graph. You can hover over individual data points to examine the connection between poverty rates and pollution levels in various regions. These two graphs show pollution levels by region, allowing you to compare and analyze the environmental impact across different geographical areas by different ecological footprints. You can select specific regions to highlight the data points in the scatter plot (and the trend line) while also modifying the visualization of the bar chart to only showcase the specific region.
